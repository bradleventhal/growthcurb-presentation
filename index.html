Board-level reality

"Your wife can’t book her nail appointment online. Neither can your customers."

If booking is hard, intent dies. Buyers move to whoever makes it easy in two taps.

What this costs local businesses

GrowthCurb fix

Every GrowthCurb build includes conversion-ready booking integration (Square/Fresha/Booksy/Acuity), plus order/quote/call paths. We remove friction at the exact moment a buyer is ready.
